# Exec Plans

Use `docs/exec-plans/active/` for in-flight work and move finished plans to `docs/exec-plans/completed/`.

Each plan should include:

- Purpose / Big Picture
- Progress
- Surprises & Discoveries
- Decision Log
- Outcomes & Retrospective
- Context and Orientation
- Plan of Work
- Concrete Steps
- Validation and Acceptance
- Idempotence and Recovery
